Absolute vs Relative Positioning



Absolute Positioning

Requirements to Use:
1. Coordinates of the elements such as top, left, right and bottom property MUST be declared
2. Element will be positioned relative to the closest positioned ancestor
3. Absolute element will be taken out of the flow of the document


Relative Positioning

1. Elements will be positioned relative to its original position



Consider the example below: There are 4 boxes which are inheriting each other. They are all aligned in the center of each other. These boxes are called Box1 through Box4; With Box1 being the largest and Box4 being the smallest. Observe the effects of Absolute and Relative Positioning in the following examples shown.
